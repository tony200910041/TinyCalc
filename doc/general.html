TinyCalc: General usage



Simply type the expression into the textbox and the result will appear immediately. Use * for multiplication, / for division and ^ for power. Leading opening bracket(s) and trailing closing bracket(s) may be omitted.

Example: (1+2*3)/4



Other examples:
79/23 3.43478 (approximately)
10^2-8^2 36
3+5)*(18-6/2 120
100+200+300)/5 120


If an error occurs during calculation, the answer label will display N/A and error information can be obtained from the tooltip text.

You may double-click the answer label to copy the answer.



If "Answer" is chosen, formatted answer like "1E20" will be copied to system clipboard.
Otherwise if "Plain value" is chosen, unformatted answer like "100000000000000000000" will be copied instead.

To close TinyCalc, simply click the close button of the frame, or type "exit".
